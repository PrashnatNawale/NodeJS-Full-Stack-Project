Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The hospital has a dedicated flow of obstetric cases in the wards and pre-labour rooms. Operation theatres and neonatal care have been provided on one floor itself. The rooms have been designed in such a way that they can be converted to fully fledged delivery suites (LDR). Once an expecting mother enters the delivery suite, she need not worry about anything till discharge.
